APPLY ONLINE

Submit your application through our online portal with required documents.

02

ENTRANCE EXAM

Appear for the university entrance examination or submit standardized test scores.

03

04

Complete enrollment formalities and secure your seat.

APPLICATION FORM

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

UNDERGRADUATE

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Entrance exam scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement

GRADUATE

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • GRE/GMAT scores
  • Professional recommendations
  • Statement of purpose
